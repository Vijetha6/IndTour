Famous Beaches of Karnataka

Om Beach in Gokarna is one of the most popular beaches in Karnataka, named after its natural Om-shaped shoreline. It is ideal for relaxation, beach walks, and water sports.

Kudle Beach, Gokarna

Kudle Beach is a serene stretch of sand located near Gokarna. It is popular among backpackers and travelers for its peaceful vibe and beachside cafes.
